# Template: Social Shorts (TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts)

## When to use

- Top-of-funnel content for B2C SaaS or prosumer tools
- Single-product moment, single outcome
- 15s, 30s, or 45s vertical video
- Assumes a cold viewer who scrolled in by accident

## Best platforms

- TikTok (15–45s)
- Instagram Reels (15–30s)
- YouTube Shorts (15–60s)
- LinkedIn (30–60s, slower pacing, less hook-bait)

## Structure (30s reference)

| Beat | Time | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| Hook | 0:00–0:03 | One painful or surprising line. No setup. No logo. |
| Problem | 0:03–0:10 | Show / name the specific friction. Make the viewer go "yes that's me." |
| Product action | 0:10–0:22 | Screen recording. One feature. Specific tap-by-tap action. |
| Result | 0:22–0:27 | Show the after-state. The thing the viewer wanted. |
| CTA | 0:27–0:30 | One ask. Direct. Concrete next step. |

## Example hooks

1. "If you've ever {painful task}, this will save you an hour a week."
2. "Stop using {common thing} for {use case}. Here's why."
3. "POV: it's {time / situation} and you're {painful state}."
4. "I built this in a weekend because {tool} kept {failing at X}."
5. "This is the {N}-second version of {expensive / annoying alternative}."

## Example CTAs

1. "Free tool — link in bio."
2. "Try it on the free plan, no card."
3. "Comment '{keyword}' and I'll DM you the link."

## Shot pattern

1. Talking-head or text-on-screen hook (1 shot, 0–3s)
2. Pain shot — old workflow / messy alternative (1 shot, 3–10s)
3. Product action — clean screen recording, slow cursor, captioned (2–3 shots, 10–22s)
4. Result — final state of the screen with on-screen text label (1 shot, 22–27s)
5. CTA — text overlay or talking head (1 shot, 27–30s)

## Script pattern

Voiceover should be ≤15 words per line. Captions on every line. Cursor movements slow and deliberate. No music vocals competing with VO.

## What to avoid

- Logo intros
- "We're excited to introduce…"
- Wide UI shots with nothing happening
- Reading the on-screen text out loud verbatim
- More than one product feature in 30 seconds
