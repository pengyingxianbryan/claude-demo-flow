# Template: Founder-Led Demo

## When to use

- Early-stage SaaS where the founder is also the face of the brand
- High-trust niches (devtools, finance, prosumer)
- Building audience alongside conversion (LinkedIn, X, YouTube)
- When the product story is more compelling than the product UI alone

## Best platforms

- LinkedIn (45–90s, talking-head + screen)
- YouTube (60–180s)
- X / Twitter (under 60s, captioned)
- Landing page hero video (45–90s)

## Structure (60s reference)

| Beat | Time | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| Founder hook | 0:00–0:05 | Talking head. One line: who you are + the painful insight. |
| The problem | 0:05–0:20 | Talking head + B-roll of old workflow. Make the pain visceral. |
| What you built | 0:20–0:50 | Screen recording with VO. One core feature, end-to-end. |
| Why it works | 0:50–0:55 | Back to talking head. One sentence on the principle. |
| CTA | 0:55–1:00 | Talking head + on-screen text. Direct ask. |

## Example hooks

1. "I'm {name}. I built {product} after {personal moment of frustration}."
2. "Three months ago I was {painful situation}. Then I built this."
3. "Most {niche} tools get one thing wrong. Here's what I did differently."
4. "I had {N} customers ask for the same thing in a week, so I shipped it."
5. "This is what I wish existed when I was {role / situation}."

## Example CTAs

1. "It's free for the first {N} users — link in bio."
2. "DM me '{keyword}' and I'll set you up."
3. "We're in beta. Comment if you want early access."

## Shot pattern

1. Talking head (camera, eye contact, no slides) — hook
2. Talking head with B-roll cut-ins of the painful old workflow
3. Clean screen recording with founder VO over the top
4. Cut back to talking head for the principle
5. Talking head + on-screen CTA text

## Script pattern

- Founder voice. First person. Specific story.
- Concrete numbers and dates beat adjectives ("3 months", "47 users", "Tuesday morning").
- One principle. Don't try to explain the whole product.
- Voiceover during screen recording should describe the *outcome*, not narrate the cursor.

## What to avoid

- Reading from a script visibly
- Generic "I'm passionate about helping…" energy
- Showing more than one product feature
- Long pitch about the company / mission
- Music that drowns out the voice
